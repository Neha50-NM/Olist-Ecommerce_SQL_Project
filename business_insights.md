Business Insights — Olist E-Commerce Analysis
Objective
The goal of this analysis is to understand customer behavior, seller performance, and operational efficiency in an e-commerce environment.

1. Customer Retention Problem
	•	Only ~3% of customers are repeat buyers
	•	Majority of users purchase only once
Insight
Customer retention is extremely low.
Business Recommendation
	•	Introduce loyalty programs
	•	Offer personalized discounts
	•	Improve post-purchase engagement

2. High-Value Customers (LTV Insight)
	•	Loyal customers (3+ orders) spend ~4x more than one-time buyers
Insight
A small group of customers contributes disproportionately to revenue.
Business Recommendation
	•	Focus on retention rather than acquisition
	•	Identify and nurture high-value customers

3. Delivery Performance Drives Satisfaction
	•	Faster deliveries lead to higher review scores
	•	Slower deliveries result in poor ratings
Insight
Delivery time is the strongest driver of customer satisfaction.
Business Recommendation
	•	Optimize logistics and shipping partners
	•	Improve delivery time predictions
	•	Penalize consistently late sellers or carriers

4. Customer Sentiment is Polarized
	•	Most reviews are either 5-star or 1-star
	•	Very few neutral reviews
Insight
Customer experience is inconsistent.
Business Recommendation
	•	Investigate causes of poor experiences
	•	Standardize service quality across sellers

5. Seller Performance Inequality
	•	Top sellers generate most of the revenue
	•	Sellers can be grouped into performance tiers
Insight
Revenue is concentrated among a small group of sellers.
Business Recommendation
	•	Reward top sellers
	•	Provide support for low-performing sellers

6. Revenue Trends
	•	Monthly revenue shows growth with fluctuations
Insight
Business performance varies over time.
Business Recommendation
	•	Identify seasonal trends
	•	Align marketing campaigns with high-growth periods

Final Takeaway
This analysis highlights that:
	•	Retention is more valuable than acquisition
	•	Delivery performance is critical for customer satisfaction
	•	A small set of customers and sellers drive most of the revenue
Improving these areas can significantly boost long-term business growth.
